In her staff accountant role she was responsible for reconciling general ledgers, tracking fixed assets with depreciation and aided in closing books for month end. Her main responsibilities in her role as a CFO is asset liability management which includes calculating weighted average rates on a monthly, quarterly and yearly basis alongside managing liquidity for loan and investment purposes. Reporting the quarterly Call Report for regulatory purposes, creating an annual budget, corresponding with federal and state regulators and presenting financial reports and projections to the board of directors. Some of her other responsibilities include supervising the teller line, bookkeeping department and her staff accountant. She is responsible for the completion of board meeting minutes that are annually reviewed by examiners.
