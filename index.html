Erinn Harris

Erinn Harris is a Chief Financial Officer (CFO) for First Citizens Bank. She has worked in the financial sector for over a decade serving in roles from teller to a staff account and working her way up to becoming a CFO.

Her education consists of an accounting degree that was obtained from Southern New Hampshire University that she earned while being a stay at home mother while her husband was serving in the United Stated Army. She graduated with a GPA of 3.92 in April of 2017. In her staff account role she was responsible for reconciling general ledgers, tracking fixed assets with depreciation and aided in closing books for month end. Her main responsibilities in her role as a CFO is asset liability management which includes calculating weighted average rates on a monthly, quarterly and yearly basis alongside managing liquidity for loan and investment purposes. Reporting the quarterly Call Report for regulatory purposes, creating an annual budget, corresponding with federal and state regulators and presenting financial reports and projections to the board of directors. Some of her other responsibilities include supervising the teller line, bookkeeping department and her staff accountant. She is responsible for the completion of board meeting minutes that are annually reviewed by examiners.

Some of her future goals include becoming a shareholder at First Citizens Bank and working toward earning her Certified Public Account certification. Which will allow her to have more opportunities in the financial sector and allow for further advancement as well as significant salary increases.
